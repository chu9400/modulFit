ModulFit: Pattern kit

The developer with 30 years of experience in vocational education and university education services has devoted himself to making pattern making easy and enjoyable. Through systematic research and practical experience, he has identified common difficulties that students face and developed ModuleFit to resolve them. This innovative approach has been patented and is contributing to the development of fashion design education.

Vision

Cocofa Corporation is a leader in the field of fashion design education. ModuleFit lowers the barriers to entry in pattern making, making it easy and fast for anyone to learn pattern making. With its flexible module structure, ModuleFit allows users to create personalized patterns for different body types and designs. Through its intuitive and user-friendly approach, ModuleFit helps users save time and costs. The mission of Cocofa Corporation is to innovate fashion design education and enable more people to realize their creative fashion designs.
